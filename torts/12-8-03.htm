Torts Class Notes 12/8/03


If you get into a lot of debate about what’s right legally, you’re probably going to go to court. You have to eventually get past the legal issues and see if you can actually settle.


Negotiation isn’t the same thing as a trial.


It isn’t realistic to come into negotiation with the same number as in your complaint.


It’s advisable for the defendant to apologize right off the bat.
