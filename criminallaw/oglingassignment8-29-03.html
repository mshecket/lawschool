Article 999. Ogling

Section 999.0.������ DEFINITIONS

In this Article, the definitions given in Section 1.13 of the Model Penal Code[1] apply unless a different meaning plainly is required.

Section 999.1.������ OGLING

A person is guilty of ogling if he engages in visual inspection of another person which is of a sexual nature or of a hostile nature based on gender that is unwelcome and repetitive or severe.[2]
