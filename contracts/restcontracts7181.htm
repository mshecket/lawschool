Restatement of Contracts, Second §§ 71 and 81


§ 71 sez that an act, forbearance, or change in a legal relation must be “sought by the promisor in exchange for his promise and given by the promisee in exchange for that promise” in order to constitute consideration.


§ 81 sez that just because the performance isn’t the thing that induced a promise doesn’t mean it doesn’t constitute consideration, and vice versa.
